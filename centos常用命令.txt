路径启动,默认在/usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx
启动：/usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx
平滑重启：/usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx -s reload
停止：/usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx -s stop
重启：/usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx -s restart
